## Creating a showcase

1. To get started, head to the [Showcase](https://app.getlancey.com/showcase) tab within Lancey.
2. Click on the `Create` button.
3. Give your showcase a name and make sure to add the demos you want in this showcase. Remember you can only add published demos to a showcase.
4. Edit the styling settings.
