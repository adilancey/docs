Showcases are a great way to combine multiple demos into a single link for a more unified experience.

<Note>This feature is only available on Growth and Enteprise plan.</Note>

Not sure what a Showcase is? Check it out [here](https://app.getlancey.com/demo/showcase/aa89b69258ec425b9958078c986e3d7c).

## Creating a Showcase

1. To get started, head to the [Showcase](https://app.getlancey.com/showcase) tab within Lancey.
2. Click on the `Create` button.
3. Give your showcase a name and make sure to add the demos you want in this showcase. 

<Info>Remember you can only add published demos to a showcase.</Info>

<Frame>
  <img height ="50%" src="/images/showcase_create.png" />
</Frame>

4. Edit the styling settings and hit `Save`.

## Sharing a Showcase

When you're done creating your Showcase, remember to share it using the links generated.

1. Click on the Showcase you created
2. Scroll to the `Share links` section
3. You'll see a direct link and embed link. Depending on your usecase, make sure to copy the appropriate link.
