<Note>Lancey Demos are no longer being supported as of August 31 2024. Read below to understand what happens next.</Note>

We regret to inform you that after August 31, 2024, Lancey Demo will no longer be supported. This decision was not made lightly, and we understand the impact it may have on your use of our product.

### Key Dates:

End of Support Date: August 31 2024


### What This Means for You:

- After August 31 2024, we will no longer provide updates, security patches, or customer support for Lancey Demos.

- You will no longer be billed for Lancey 

- You will no longer have access to demos created


### Transition Plan

We've partnered with Navattic to offer you continuing support for your interactive demo needs as Lancey approaches our end-of-life date of August 31st, 2024. 

Navattic offers a startup plan starting at <b>$50/month</b>. 
It includes:
- unlimited interactive demos
- unlimited seats
- unlimited demo viewers. 

To get started, you may sign up using the link below.

Throughout this transition and onwards, the Navattic team is available to answer any questions you may have. Feel free to schedule a call with their team [here](https://calendly.com/zoe-stilphen/30min).
