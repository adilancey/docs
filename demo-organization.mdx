It doesn't take long before you've gotten trigger happy and created more demos than you know what to do with. Well this ones for you.

Instead of letting your demos run wild, you can create folders to better organize the content.

### Creating and managing folders

1. Head to [demo overview](https://app.getlancey.com/demos) screen and click the `Create folder` button. Give your folder a name and hit `Save`.

2. Once you're created a folder, you can now move a demo within a folder.
3. Find a demo you want to move and click on the `Move demo` button.

4. Select the folder and click the `Move in` button.
