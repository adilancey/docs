---
title: 'Adding and removing users'
description: 'Manage users within your company and their access levels.'
---

<Note> Only Admins can add or remove users. </Note>

Navigate to the [Users](https://app.getlancey.com/settings/users) section from the Settings menu. You'll see a table of all current users in the system.

## Adding users

1. Click on the `Add user` button on the top right.
2. Fill in the `Name`, `Email` along with their `Role`. You can add them as either an Admin or as a Member.

<Info> If you're not sure if someone should be an Admin, default them to Member role unless required. Limit the number of Admins in your company. </Info>

## Deleting users

1. To remove a user, find their name on the table and click the `Edit` button
2. Click on the `Delete Account` button. Be careful to not delete someone by mistake, that's never fun.
