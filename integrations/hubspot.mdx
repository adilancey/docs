---
title: "Hubspot"
---

Our Hubspot integration allows you to send leads captured in Lancey directly as a contacts within your CRM.

To get started, navigate to the Integrations page and follow the instructions below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Head to integrations">
    Open up the [integrations](https://app.getlancey.com/integrations) page from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on Hubspot">
    Find the `Hubspot` card on the page and hit the `Connect` button.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Hubspot to Lancey">
    You'll see an option to select your Hubspot account and connect Lancey
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once the integration is setup, all leads captured via the Lancey form will automatically show up in Hubspot.

By default Lancey will populate few Hubspot fields such as `First Name`, `Last Name`, `Email`.

Additional properties captured will also be added to the contact under a section called `Lancey`.
