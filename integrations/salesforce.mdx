title: "Salesforce"
---

Our Salesforce integration allows you to send leads captured in Lancey directly as a contacts within your CRM.

To get started, navigate to the Integrations page and follow the instructions below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Head to integrations">
    Open up the [integrations](https://app.getlancey.com/integrations) page from the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on Salesforce">
    Find the `Salesforce` card on the page and hit the `Connect` button.
<Frame>
  <img height ="20%" src="/images/salesforce_integration.png" />
</Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Salesforce to Lancey">
    You'll see an option to select your Salesforce account and connect Lancey directly using OAuth connector.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once the integration is setup, all leads captured via the Lancey form will automatically show up in Salesforce.

<Note>By default Lancey will populate Salesforce fields such as `First Name`, `Last Name`, `Email` and add Lancey as the lead source for the lead so you can track where it originated from.</Note>
