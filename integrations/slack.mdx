title: "Slack"
---

Our Slack integration allows you to stay on top of your demo analytics directly within Slack.

To get started, navigate to the Integrations page and follow the instructions below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Head to integrations">
    Open up the [integrations](https://app.getlancey.com/integrations) page from the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on Slack">
    Find the `Salesforce` card on the page and hit the `Connect` button.
<Frame>
  <img height ="20%" src="/images/slack_integration.png" />
</Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Slack to Lancey">
    You'll see an option to select your Slack account. Hit the `Confirm` button.
    <Frame>
  <img height ="20%" src="/images/slack_connector.png" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Setup Slack channels">
    Once the Slack integration is connected, all that's left is to toggle the `Send leads to Slack` option and select the channel you want get notified in.

    For this step, we recommend creating a new public channel where you want Lancey to notify you and add your team members.
    <Frame>
  <img height ="20%" src="/images/slack_channel.png" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once the integration is setup, you'll start seeing notifications in the selected Slack channel.
