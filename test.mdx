<span style='color:rgb(107, 114, 128)'><b>Capturing a new demo</b> </span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Let's go through turning your application into an interactive demo.</span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Before you begin, make sure you’ve installed the</span> <a href='https://docs.lancey.io/chrome-extension'>Chrome extension</a><span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>.</span>

### <span id="h.12d4u0b36f09"><span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'><b>Decide what you want to capture</b> </span></span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Since Lancey can create a pixel-free interactive demo of your product, its helpful to decide what feature or flows you want to cover as part of your demo.</span>

### <span id="h.srdj4txj01ip"><a href='https://docs.lancey.io/capture-demo#open-the-lancey-extension'><b>​</b> </a></span>

### <span id="h.srdj4txj01ip"><span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'><b>Open the Lancey extension</b> </span></span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Find the Lancey extension in your Chrome address bar. Remember to pin the extension so its easy to access at anytime.</span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Once you’ve opened it, you’ll see it pop up on your screen.</span>

### <span id="h.vkhe2ddnrak4"><a href='https://docs.lancey.io/capture-demo#start-the-capture-process'><b>​</b> </a></span>

### <span id="h.vkhe2ddnrak4"><span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'><b>Start the capture process</b> </span></span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>All that’s left to do is hit the</span> <span style='color:rgb(17, 24, 39)'>Create Demo</span> <span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>button.</span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Once you begin the process, Lancey will follow you across the demo and keep track of how many frames you’ve recorded. The extension will show up on the bottom left hand of the screen.</span>

![https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/63a0bb4c1e9b0d75c72746e6/images/63b47b2ca199b514924d92e6/file-vVHi9BYLh7.png](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/VvtnM7g2C3ktqNEm3wrTKuNtrgV6Lta2Fw2ETXhtlsa2I_h81fDYdzZEws4K7nnurHUKtPApVHhOWRaT0v8oULv1yUj7vfYDni8_71YodqujcHb33KbuWVMIP6XiqpMrJ_w8VVnQ6La-2MnTrF1_9i-NlfEQXR5z "")


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Lancey will only capture a frame when you click, type, or interact with the page. It is not a video recording tool.</span>

### <span id="h.cyd7tneida6o"><a href='https://docs.lancey.io/capture-demo#finishing-the-recording'><b>​</b> </a></span>

### <span id="h.cyd7tneida6o"><span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'><b>Finishing the recording</b> </span></span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>Once you’re done capturing your perfect demo, you can hit the</span> <span style='color:rgb(17, 24, 39)'>Stop recording</span> <span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>button on the extension</span> <span style='color:rgb(17, 24, 39)'>located on the bottom left hand of the screen</span><span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>.</span>


<span style='color:rgb(55, 65, 81)'>After the recording is complete, you’ll get redirected to a new tab with your interactive demo.</span>
