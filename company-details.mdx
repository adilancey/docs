<Note> Only Admins can make changes to Company settings. </Note>

Navigate to the [Company](https://app.getlancey.com/settings/company) section from the Settings menu.

## Updating company logo

<Frame>
  <img height ="50%" src="/images/org_settings.png" />
</Frame>

Every demo will have the Lancey logo in the start, end, and lead capture screen. You can update this to match your company's logo.

<Info>Logo files must be in PNG, JPEG or SVG format. </Info>

## Changing company name

Change the name to reflect your company. By default the company name will be based on the domain of your account.
